FAQ'S

1. How does Algo Trading work?

An algorithm analyzes market data in real-time and automatically executes buy and sell orders when market conditions are met.

2. What is Algo Trading?

Algo trading uses automated, pre-programmed instructions to execute trades based on conditions like price, timing, and volume, reducing human error and execution delays.

3. Is Algo Trading faster than manual trading?

Yes, algo trading is much faster, executing trades automatically and at higher speeds than human traders, enabling high-frequency trading.

4. Do I need to know Algorithmic/Algo Trading to work with AQI?

No! AQI is designed for beginners—no knowledge of algorithms required. It provides processed information that’s easy to use.

5. Is Algo Trading only for experienced traders?

Algo trading is accessible to everyone—both experienced and retail traders. It’s a powerful tool for sustainable trading, with educational resources available to help beginners.

6. Do I need to know Technical Analysis to work with AQI?

Not necessary. AQI delivers ready-to-use technical analysis outputs, but users with technical analysis knowledge can enhance their experience.

7. What if my system crashes? Will I get a reinstallation?

No worries! AQI is cloud-based, so simply log in from any device—no installation required.

8. Is it possible to use multiple algo trading platforms simultaneously?

Yes, you can use multiple platforms to run different trading strategies and leverage various features.

9. Will you take requests for customization of the product?

Yes, we welcome feedback and can incorporate feasible customization requests to improve the product.
